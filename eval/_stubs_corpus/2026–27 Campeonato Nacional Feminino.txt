The 2026–27 Campeonato Nacional Femenino, known as the 2026–27 Liga BPI for sponsorship reasons, will be the 42nd season of the Campeonato Nacional Feminino, the top division of women's football in Portugal.
Benfica are the defending champions, having won their sixth title in the 2025–26 season.
