Adeola Chizoba Adeyemi, known professionally as Diiadem, is a Nigerian reality star and businesswoman, best known for her role in the AMVCA nominated hit TV show The Real Housewives of Lagos and her makeup brand Beauty by AD.


== Early Life & Education ==
Diiadem was born in Lagos, Nigeria, on April 12, 1991, and is of Yoruba-Igbo heritage. She studied at Eben Noble Primary School, Bariga, and Saka Tinubu Memorial High School, Agege, both in Lagos State. She earned her Bachelor’s degree in Arts at Sikkim Manipal University, Accra, Ghana. She went on to receive formal training as a makeup artist at the Make-Up Designory in California, United States.


== Career ==
Diiadem began her career as a video vixen, model, and content creator.
She founded the cosmetic brand Beauty by AD in 2016, inspired by her time as a make-up artist, to address the inclusion gaps she observed in the market with products for deeper skin tones. With the brand’s success, she started Face of Beauty by AD (FOBAD) in August 2019, aimed at empowering young women in their modeling careers. Beauty by AD signed its first ambassador, 43rd Miss Nigeria and reality star, Beauty Tukura in 2022, releasing a highlighter palette called “Shimmer Cruise” to mark the collaboration.


=== Content Creation & Brand Influencing ===
In January 2021, Diiadem was unveiled as a brand ambassador for Hush'D Makeover, and later for fashion brand Zeena & Zara in June of the same year. She also appeared as an ambassador for Lush Hair, walking the Lagos Fashion Week runway alongside Denrele Edun, Shaffy Bello and more in 2021.


=== Reality TV ===
In January 2025, Diiadem was unveiled as a new cast member in the third season of The Real Housewives of Lagos (RHOLagos). She has since become a favorite on the show. She is  known for her thoughts on getting married, saying, "I'm single and I'm not single, that is, until he puts a ring on it."


=== Magazine Covers and Features ===
Diiadem was featured as the Cover Star of Exquisite Magazine in April 2022, and La Mode Magazine's 83rd Edition in July 2023. In June 2025, she was featured on Marie Claire Nigeria, discussing her growth from a makeup lover to a beauty mogul.


== Personal Life ==
Diiadem is a single parent to her daughter, Akorede (Koko or Koks). She has stated that she left her daughter's father due to domestic abuse while she was pregnant.


== Awards ==
On October 1, 2020, Diiadem won the La Mode Award for Social Media Brand Influencer of the Year. She was named as one of Leading Ladies Africa’s Most Inspiring Women of 2023.
In March 2025, she, alongside Dabota Lawson, represented the Nigerian Beauty Industry at the United Kingdom House of Lords. She was also nominated for Entrepreneur of the year 2025 at the Masters of Industry Award.
