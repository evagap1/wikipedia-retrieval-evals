Alexander Ronald McDonnell (May 9, 1901 – May 6, 1989) was a Republican member of the Montana Legislature. He was elected to the Montana Senate in 1953 to represent Senate District 19, which at the time included Sweet Grass County and Park County. McDonnell served four consecutive terms as a Senator, retiring from the role in 1965. McDonnell also served a the Chairman of Citizens Bank & Trust Co, a bank which his father, Charles McDonnell had started.


== Personal life ==
In McDonnell's personal life, he married Ellen Rein in 1941 and the two had one child, Mary Ellen (Mimi) McDonnell. Mimi is currently serving as the chair of Citizens Bank and Trust Co, following in her father's footsteps.
McDonnell died on May 6, 1989, at the age of 87.
