Jean Pierre Biyiti bi Essam (20 July 1949 – 23 April 2026) was a Cameroonian politician and diplomat.


== Life and career ==
Jean Pierre Biyiti bi Essam was born in Ebolowa on 20 July 1949. He served as the Minister of Posts and Telecommunications from June 30, 2009 to October 1, 2015. He served as the Ambassador of Cameroon to Israel from 31 May 2018 until his death in 2026.
Biyiti bi Essam died in Tel Aviv on 23 April 2026, at the age of 76.
