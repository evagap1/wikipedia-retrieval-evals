Founded in 2008 by Jeffrey Rudes, who currently serves as Chairman and Creative Director, L'Agence is a Los Angeles-based fashion label best known for its line of denim, along with outerwear, swimwear, shoes and accessories. Beginning with one location on Melrose Place in Los Angeles for its first decade, the company has since launched flagship locations in New York City in 2018, Beverly Hills and Malibu in 2023, Paris and Seoul, as well as stores in Newport Beach and Houston. Under Fashion Director Tara Rudes Dann, L’Agence is carried in stores worldwide, including Saks Fifth Avenue, Nordstrom, Neiman Marcus and Revolve. 
On top of its line of denim, L'Agence has converted its original Melrose location to a store that exclusively sells jeans. Additionally, L'Agence CEO Jonny Saven is Co-Founder and Lead Advisor for the Denim Institute and Museum, a non-profit dedicated to preserving denim culture via exhibits, classes and training. 
In 2026, L'Agence entered into a partnership with Google to utilize the tech company’s virtual try-on technology, via Gemini, at New York Fashion Week.
