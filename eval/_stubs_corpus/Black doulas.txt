
== Black Doulas ==
Black Doulas are non-clinically trained Black professionals who provide maternal support for pregnant women, particularly within Black communities. This support is provided throughout the entire pregnancy and into the postpartum phase after childbirth. They are often discussed in the context of promoting the health of pregnant black women because they are disproportionately affected by the maternal mortality rate (Maternal mortality in the United States). They do this by addressing the disparities in Systemic racism in maternal health and by adhering to the mother they are working with to help with any factor that could be a pregnancy-related issues. Pregnancies with doulas are associated with a better birth experience and outcomes, especially within Black women.
