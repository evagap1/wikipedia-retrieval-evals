Schizonycha debilis is a species of beetle of the family Scarabaeidae. It is found in South Africa (Eastern Cape).


== Description ==
Adults reach a length of about 15–16 mm (0.59–0.63 in). They are testaceous with head and pronotum darker. This species shows strong sexual dimorphism. Females have much reduced wings and a very short antennal club.
