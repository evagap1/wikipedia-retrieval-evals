Kerry Webber (born c.1967), is a former international boxer from Wales who won a bronze medal at the Commonwealth Games.


== Biography ==
Webber was a member of the Rhondaa Amateur Boxing Club, boxed at flyweight and was the 1986 Welsh champion.
Webber represented the Welsh team at the 1986 Commonwealth Games in Edinburgh, Scotland, where he competed in the flyweight division. He won a bronze medal following his semi-final defeat against Leonard Makhanya of Kenya. 
Soon after the Games he turned professional, making his professional debut in December 1986. After a year out of boxing he made a comeback at bantamweight in April 1988.
