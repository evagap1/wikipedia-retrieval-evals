Sigríður Ósk Hrafnkelsdóttir (born 15 April 1999), also known as Sigga Ózk, is an Icelandic singer and beauty pageant titleholder who won Miss Iceland 2026. She will represent Iceland at Miss Universe 2026.


== Early life and career ==
Sigga was born on 15 April to Hrafnkell Pálmarsson and Elín Mária Björnsdóttir, and was raised in Garðabær.
She holds a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) degree, as well as a diploma in composition from the Lillehammer Institute for Music Production and Industries.
She competed in Iceland's national selection, Söngvakeppnin, in 2023 and 2024, reaching the final on both occasions. Her song "Gleyma þér og dansa" was released by RÚV.


== Pageantry ==


=== Miss Iceland ===
Sigga represented Hafravatn at the Miss Iceland 2026 pageant. She was crowned Miss Iceland 2026 (Icelandic: Ungfrú Ísland 2026) on 22 April 2026 at Gamla Bíó in Reykjavík.


=== Miss Universe ===

She will represent Iceland at the Miss Universe 2026 competition, which is scheduled to take place in November 2026 in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
