Jane Snow (born 3 February 1993), known mononymously as Limbo is a singer known for her hit song Airplane Mode. 


== Personal life ==
Snow has two brothers. She keeps her real face anonymous under a cat mask, which she is mostly recognised for. She has stated she is pansexual. Her stage name is derivited from the dance and the fact she was struck with “a fluid state of limbo”.


== Career ==
She originally began her career as a DJ but realised she needed to make music for her sets, as she wanted people to dance to her own songs so she began with YouTube tutorials.
She began making music with song “Late Night Illuminati Talks” and later made 2016’s hit song “Airplane Mode”.
In 2018, she released her debut album, Holo, which was after she left her label at the time and went independent. In the same year, she released several projects such as the EP “Catskill” and “All Of Us” before releasing her second album “Cloud Nine” in 2019.
The album was preceded by one single: “U Know Only U” and the album later released on 10th January 2019.
From then, she continued making music and posting it on SoundCloud until she announced a hiatus in 2021 and released many of her songs onto streaming services on 9 June 2021.
She later went back to music with single “Inside” and has been continuing releasing music ever since, her most recent release as of April 2026 being “Snow” (stylised in lowercase).
