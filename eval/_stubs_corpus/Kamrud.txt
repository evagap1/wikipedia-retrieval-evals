Kamrud (Persian: كامرود) is a village in Seh Qaleh Rural District of Seh Qaleh District in Sarayan County, South Khorasan province, Iran.


== Demographics ==


=== Population ===
At the time of the 2006 and 2011 National Censuses, the village's population was below the reporting threshold. The 2016 census measured the population of the village as 28 people in nine households.
