Onuralp Çevikkan (born 2 January 2006) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Süper Lig club Göztepe.


== Club career ==
A youth product of Altınordu, Çevikkan transferred to Trabzonspor on 1 July 2023 on a 3-year contract. On 3 December 2025, he made his professional debut with Trabzonspor in a 2–0 Turkish Cup win over Vanspor.


== International career ==
Çevikkan is a youth international Türkiye, and was called up to the Türkiye U19s in September 2024.
