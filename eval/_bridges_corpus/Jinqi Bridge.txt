The Jinqi Bridge (Chinese: 金旗特大桥) is a bridge between Qingzhen and Xiuwen County, Guizhou, China over the Maotiao River.
With a height of 300.6 metres (986 ft), the north tower is one of the tallest bridge structure in the world, it is also one of the highest bridge in the world with a deck 258 metres (846 ft) above the river.
The span arrangement is 169–320–169 metres (554–1,050–554 ft).
