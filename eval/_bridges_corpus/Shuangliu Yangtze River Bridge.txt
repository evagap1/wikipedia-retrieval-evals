The Shuangliu Yangtze River Bridge (Chinese: 双柳长江大桥) is a suspension bridge under construction over the Yangtze river between Wuhan and Ezhou in China. The bridge is one of the longest suspension bridges with a main span of 1,430 m (4,690 ft).
