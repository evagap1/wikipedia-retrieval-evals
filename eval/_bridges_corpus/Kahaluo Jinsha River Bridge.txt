The Kahaluo Jinsha River Bridge (Chinese: 卡哈洛金沙江大桥) is a suspension bridge under construction over the Jinsha River between Leibo County, Sichuan and Yongshan County, Yunnan, China. The bridge is one of the longest suspension bridges with a main span of 1,030 m (3,380 ft). The Xiluodu Dam domwnstream has increased the height of the Jinsha River.
