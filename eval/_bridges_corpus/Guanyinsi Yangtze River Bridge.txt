The Guanyinsi Yangtze River Bridge (Chinese: 观音寺长江大桥) is a cable-stayed bridge spanning the Yangtze River near Gong'an County, Hubei in China. It will be the second longest cable-stayed bridge in the world when completed with a main span of 1,160 m (3,810 ft).
The bridge is about 31.3 kilometres (19.4 mi) from the upstream Jingzhou Yangtze River Bridge and about 13.7 kilometres (8.5 mi) from the lower Gong'an Yangtze River Bridge.
