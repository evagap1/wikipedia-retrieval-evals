The Fuxing Yangtze River Bridge (Chinese: 复兴长江大桥) is a suspension bridge under construction over the Yangtze river in Yunyang County, Chongqing, China. The bridge is one of the longest suspension bridges with a main span of 1,208 m (3,963 ft).
