Cose stupide (transl. Stupid things) is a song recorded by Italian singer Alessandra Amoroso. It was released on 4 April 2025 by Epic as the fifth single from her eighth studio album, Io non sarei.


== Description ==
The song was written by Daniele Fossatelli, Leonardo Zaccaria, and Pietro Celona, who also handled the production.


== Promotion ==
The song was performed by the singer herself on 5 April 2025 during the third evening episode of the twenty-fourth edition of the talent show Amici di Maria De Filippi.


== Music video ==
The music video, directed by Marco Braia, was released alongside the song on the Amoroso's YouTube channel and featured Italian actress Matilde Gioli.
