"In Another World" is the debut solo single by South Korean and American singer-songwriter Ejae. After teasing the song on her Instagram, the song was released on October 24, 2025. The official music video to the song was released a day prior on October 23, 2025, via Ejae's YouTube channel. It is her first solo release since her role as the singing voice of Rumi in KPop Demon Hunters. It is also her first release after signing with WME.
Ejae says she wrote the song two years ago while at a songwriting camp in Canada, about her relationship with her fiancé during a period of it "not working out", eventually leading to them taking a break. The song, lyrically, is Ejae reflecting on how the relationship would've gone under different circumstances. She said in an interview with Variety that the lyrics "helped me accept reality in a way, but at the same time, be OK with it by knowing to just be real with yourself. I chose this song because it felt exactly how I felt with Rumi and the movie. Accepting your demons, then accepting that part of yourself — that is when true growth happens."
